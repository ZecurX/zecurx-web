## 5. Legal , Compliance Assurance & Program Ethics

- Our internship programs are designed with compliance, transparency, and ethical practices as a
priority. This is a training-based internship program where participants enroll by paying a program
fee. Clear program agreements define scope, duration, learning outcomes, responsibilities, IP usage,
and confidentiality. The program is learning-focused and does not replace full-time employment
roles, and aligns with applicable training and labor guidelines where relevant.
- Data Protection & Confidentiality:All participants sign confidentiality and non-disclosure
agreements prior to onboarding. Access to systems, project materials, and data is limited to training
purposes and handled in accordance with standard data protection and access-control practices.

~Note: Final legal and compliance alignment may be reviewed with relevant advisors or institutional
guidelines.
