**COOKIE POLICY**

This Cookie Policy explains how **ZecurX Cybersecurity Pvt. Ltd.** uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience and secure our digital platforms.

**1. What Are Cookies?**

Cookies are small data files stored on a user's device to support essential functionality and improve browsing experience.

**2. Types of Cookies We Use**

-   **Essential Cookies:** Required for core website functions such as authentication and page navigation.

-   **Analytics Cookies:** Help us analyze website performance, traffic, and user behavior.

-   **Security Cookies:** Detect malicious activity and protect system integrity.

-   **Preference Cookies:** Remember user preferences such as language or login details.

**3. Why We Use Cookies**

Cookies enable us to:

-   Provide secure and stable platform access

-   Improve performance and usability

-   Track issues and enhance cybersecurity monitoring

-   Personalize content and user experience

**4. Managing Cookies**

Users may disable cookies through browser settings; however, certain features may become unavailable or function incorrectly.

**5. Third-Party Cookies**

We may use trusted services such as analytics or monitoring platforms that deploy their own cookies.
