**Enterprise Security Capabilities**

ZecurX delivers a unified enterprise security platform designed to protect modern organizations across endpoints, cloud, identity, applications, and data---powered by AI, automation, and real-world threat intelligence.

Each capability is built to reduce risk, simplify operations, and improve security outcomes at scale.

**Endpoint Security**

**What problem this solves**\
Endpoints remain the most exploited entry point for attackers, especially with remote work, BYOD, and unmanaged devices.

**How ZecurX does it differently**\
ZecurX provides continuous endpoint visibility and behavior-based protection rather than relying only on signatures.

**AI & automation angle**\
AI models detect abnormal behavior, lateral movement, and zero-day activity, while automated workflows isolate compromised devices instantly.

**Enterprise outcomes**

-   Reduced breach probability

-   Faster incident containment

-   Lower operational overhead for SOC teams
